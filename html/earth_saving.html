SAVING THE EARTH

HERE ARE SOME OF THE EXISTING MEASURES THAT HAVE BEEN DONE

#teamtrees

American YouTuber MrBeast started the #teamtrees campaign in 2019. The goal of the campaign was to raise funds to plant 20 million trees by the end of the year. A dollar contributed added one tree to be planted. The campaign reached it's target by the end of 2019.

#teamseas

With the success of #teamtrees, another campaign was started, called #teamseas. The goal of this campaign was to 30 million pounds of platic and debris from the world's oceans. MrBeast partnered with The Ocean Cleanup, a non-profit organisation dedicated to developing technologies to removing plastic waste from the oceans. The campaign also aimed to raise funds through donations to support cleanup efforts. It received worldwide support, and funds raised were used to develop technologies for effective ocean cleaup.
